Second consecutive year of HPCI/Fugaku allocation (FY2026)

February 19, 2026

I’m pleased to share that my research project has been selected for HPCI computational resources on the supercomputer Fugaku for the second consecutive year.

For FY2026 (Period-A), the awarded Fugaku General Access project is:

  • Project ID: hp260140 
  • Title: From Validation to Application: Advancing the Super-Droplet Method and Evaluating Weather Intervention Strategies for Extreme Rainfall Controllability in Japan
  • Awarded resources: 4,325,000 node hours (RIKEN R-CCS, Supercomputer Fugaku)

This award will support large-scale simulations to further advance the Super-Droplet Method and to evaluate strategies related to extreme rainfall controllability in Japan.
